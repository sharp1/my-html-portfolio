The Best Movies According to Terry

My top 3 movies of all time.

Do The Right Thing

A movie by Spike Lee, set in the Bedstuy section of Brooklyn, New York that explores issues of race.

Man on Fire

An action movie that stars Denzel Washington as a washed up Field Agent struggling to be at peace with himself, he meets a family and a little girl who brings a spark in his life, this girl is kidnapped and Denzel's character must polish off his old skills of going after the bad guys.
